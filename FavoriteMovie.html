Favorite Movie

Don't Think Twice

More information: Don't Think Twice official page

Why?

I don't really have a favorite movie, but this is one of the best movies I have seen in a while. This film had some of the bes humor that I have seen in a film. When I went into see the film it wasn't exactly what I expected, but it suprised me and was a great film.

Favorite Actor

My favorite actor in the film was Keegan Michael Key. He did a great job, along with all of the other actors in the film, of getting into character whenever he was on stage. He also did a great job of switching between characters and made his character seem very convincing.
